## Observation
During analysis of enterprise infrastructure failures, loss of
connectivity resulted in the unavailability of dependent internal
systems and management tools.

## Impact
The outage prevented normal remote troubleshooting and significantly
extended recovery time due to reliance on alternative access methods.

## Mitigation Considerations
- Isolate recovery and management access from primary network paths
- Validate configuration changes before deployment
- Reduce single points of failure in control mechanisms
